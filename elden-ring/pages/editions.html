Elden Ring DLC Rumors

Will Elden Ring get DLC or an expansion in the future?

So you've finally become the Elden Lord and ushered in a new age for The Lands Between. Now what? Elden Ring offers a New Game+ mode in the form of “New Journies” but that's basically the same content just ramped up in difficulty.

For those looking for new experiences, we'll likely have to wait for DLC and an expansion. But will we get one?

Are there plans for Elden Ring DLC or expansions?

As of right now, FromSoftware has not officially announced any plans for DLC or expansions for Elden Ring. But given the game's success and popularity, and FromSoftware's history with Souls game, it's not unreasonable to expect some form of post-launch content. What that will look like exactly remains a mystery though.

Perhaps the biggest source of evidence tied to Elden Ring DLC is a YouTube video from popular Souls content creator Lance McDonald. In his unlisted video, Lance investigates a locked coliseum located in the starting zone of Limgrave. By fiddling with a camera mod, he's actually able to get inside and, shockingly, discovers that it's actually modeled. The entire inside looks like a PvP arena, similar to the Brotherhood of Blood dueling arena from Dark Souls 2. Dark Souls 3 also received its PvP arena via DLC.

The speculation is this will be unlocked as future DLC for the game, allowing players to more easily fight each other. Right now if you approach the coliseum, a red NPC invades you.

This isn't exactly confirmation that Elden Ring is getting DLC, but it certainly is intriguing. Why would FromSoftware model the entire inside of this coliseum if there were not plans to utilize it in the future? Personally, I'm not a huge fan of PvP in Elden Ring, or any of the Souls games, so hopefully if FromSoftware does have plans for DLC it's more than just this coliseum.
