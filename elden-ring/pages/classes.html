ELDEN RING CLASSES OVERVIEW

Classes for Elden Ring are the starting archetypes available to the player. All Elden Ring Classes follow similar concept to that of the Dark Souls series, choosing a class determines the player's starting Stats and Equipment which can then be improved and upgraded later on as you progress throughout the game.

Players can select a Keepsake (like Gifts of Dark Souls) during Character Creation, which provide more customization options during early game.
